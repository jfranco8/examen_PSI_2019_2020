Instructions

Welcome to Cats vs Mouse!!

The game is made for two players, one will be the cats and the other the mouse. Cats moves first, then players alternate their moves. The cat player begins the game with 4 cats, and the mouse player with one mouse.

To move, each player places one of their pieces on a diagonal white squares close to that piece, the cats are able to move only forward, but the mouse can move both forward and backwards.

The aim of the game is for the cats to catch the mouse so it has no possible moves to make and for the mouse to overstep the cats or get to the other side of the board.
